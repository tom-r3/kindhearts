Services We Provide

Kind Hearts provides a wide range of personal care, home management, transportation, and respite services. We help with daily activities and provide assistance for seniors in our community.

Our specialized training allows us to provide a person centred approach to caring for those living with dementia. Our goal is to enhance life through kindness by facilitating calm, positive and meaningful experiences.

Helping is our passion and we customize our service based on the specific needs of each client. We can help as much or as little as you want, on a regular or as-needed basis.

Our Services

Kind Hearts Cares

We provide compassionate, high quality care and support for seniors. Our purpose is:

  • To promote independence and the ability to remain living at home for as long as possible

  • To enrich the lives of those living with dementia by providing calm, nurturing experiences

  • To assist in recovery after a discharge from hospital or while undergoing treatment

  • To provide support and relief to family caregivers and to help maintain balance within today’s busy lifestyles
