Kind Hearts cares.

We help with daily activities and provide assistance for seniors in our community.

Our purpose is:

  • To promote independence and the ability to remain living at home for as long as possible

  • To enrich the lives of those living with dementia by providing calm, nurturing experiences

  • To assist in recovery after a discharge from hospital or while undergoing treatment

  • To provide support and relief to family caregivers and to help maintain balance within today’s busy lifestyles

Our Promise

Services We Provide

Kind Hearts provides a wide range of household, social and transportation services that can be tailored to meet your individual needs – we customize our service based on individual needs. We can help as much or as little as you want, on a regular or as-needed basis.
