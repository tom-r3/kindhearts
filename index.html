Services We Provide

Kind Hearts provides exceptional care for seniors and offers an array of services to meet changing needs and circumstances. Reliable assistance is provided with compassion and integrity.

We help individuals or couples to remain living in their own homes as long as possible; We facilitate transitions to assisted living residences; We enhance routine care by providing a competent service, meaningful visits and nurturing experiences; We offer an invaluable resource for family members living in other communities.

Our specialized training and passion allows us to provide a person centred and individual approach to caring for those living with Mild Cognitive Impairment, Alzheimer’s, and other neurocognitive disorders. We assist with daily routines and focus on enriching life by facilitating calm, positive and meaningful experiences.

Helping is our purpose and we customize our service based on the specific needs and preferences of each client. Care is always provided in a safe, positive and friendly manner.

Our Services

Kind Hearts Cares

We provide reliable, high quality care and support for seniors. Our goals are:

  • To promote health, safety and wellbeing with a focus on reliability, compassion and kindness

  • To make things easier for seniors by helping and performing tasks that become difficult and overwhelming with age

  • To provide assistance within families when time or distance put a strain on resources
