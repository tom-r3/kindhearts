Services

Kind Hearts: A Service for Seniors

Home Services Services

Kind Hearts provides a wide range of services tailored to meet individual needs and preferences. We are happy to help on a regular, as needed, or one-time basis. Overnight care can also be arranged. We are situated in Cambridge, Ontario and offer assistance to seniors living in surrounding areas. Please click on a service below to learn more.

Personal Care

Dignity, preferences, respect, and privacy are of the utmost importance when we assist clients with personal hygiene needs. We encourage independence while assisting with personal care including bathing, skin care, grooming, dressing, toileting, and mobility. Light exercise and range of motion activities are incorporated into personal care routines.

Specialized Care for MCI, Alzheimer’s etc.

Monitor routines; Setup visual cues and reminders; Create and update wall calendar; Post family and emergency contacts; Check for safety hazards; Setup wandering safeguards; Encourage and assist with personal hygiene; Simplify food and Meal Plans; Physical activity; Music experiences; Reminiscing; Meaningful conversation; Enhance self-esteem; Group activities (Alzheimer Society and Community); Supportive social outings; Board games; Puzzles; Hobbies.

Exercise and Socializing

Outdoor walks; Indoor track or shopping mall walks during inclement weather; Visit parks; Neighbourhood walks; Group fitness and stretching programs; Conditioning centres; Low impact exercise activities; Fundraising walks; Community events; Senior Centre programs. Organize social outings; Assist and encourage participation in activities; Self development workshops; Personal interest classes; Shopping; Markets; Religious Services; Theatre; Movies; Museums; Art Shows; Sporting events; Dining out; Group activities; Visit friends and family; Cooking & eating meals together; Discuss current and historical events; Reminiscing; Read books/newspapers; Correspondence; Board games; Cards; Puzzles; Crafts.

Medical Appointment Assistance

Schedule specific and recurring appointments including Medical, Dental, Lab Work, Hospital treatments/tests, Massage, Physiotherapy, etc; Transportation to appointments; Support during appointments; Document information; Ask pertinent questions; Monitor prescriptions; Followup procedures & appointments; Instruction reminders; Report information and results to family members.

Shopping and Errands

Grocery Stores; Clothing/Shoe Stores; Department Stores; Specialty Stores; Shopping Malls; Pharmacy; Post Office; Library; Dry Cleaners; Pet Supplies; Hardware Stores; Hair Salon/Barber Shop; Gift buying. NOTE: We can shop for you or with you and assist with ambulation as needed.

Home Management

Light housekeeping; Laundry; Change linens; Ironing; Organize closets, cupboards and drawers; Water houseplants; Basic sewing and mending; Empty trash; Replace light bulbs/batteries; Assist with electronic equipment; Take recycling and trash to curb; Shovel and salt walkways; Plant, water and weed gardens; Fill bird feeders; Pickup and sort mail; Schedule appointments; Organize calendar; Help with computers & email; Assist with correspondence; Bill payment/reminders; Arrange home deliveries; Oversee home maintenance; Plan outings and visits; Help with entertaining; Assist with pet care; Gift wrapping; Holiday decorating.

Meal and Food Preparation

Monitor diet and eating; Plan meals; Make grocery lists; Grocery shopping; Meal preparation; Cleanup; Fruit and vegetable cleaning; Baking; Batch cooking for freezer; Monitor food spoilage and expiration.

Family Respite Care

We strive to sustain family caregiver health and wellbeing by providing emotional support and temporary care for loved ones. Specific needs and instructions will be followed and all of our services are offered. It can be difficult, and guilt often plays a role, but family caregivers must look after themselves and take time to recharge in order to care for others.

Recovery Support

Assist nurses and other health care team members by following the established care plan; Encourage and assist with physiotherapy exercises; Help with passive or active-assistive range of motion exercises and other rehabilitative measures; Monitor food and fluid intake; Measure and report vital signs; Clean equipment; Observe and report progress, status and changes to the healthcare team and/or family members.

Daily Checks

Daily visits or phone calls to help ensure safety, good health and wellbeing. Regular contact reduces depression associated with isolation in winter months or when mobility issues causes limitations. This service provides peace of mind when family members live out of town, travel for business, or take a refreshing holiday.

And More

If you would like assistance with anything that is not on our list of services, please contact us and we will do our best to accommodate your needs. If we are unable to help you, we will attempt to find other resources.
