Services

Kind Hearts: A Service for Seniors

Home Services Services

Kind Hearts provides a wide range of household, social and transportation services that can be tailored to meet your individual needs. We are happy to help you on a regular, as needed, or one-time basis. We are situated in Cambridge, Ontario and offer our assistance to those living in the Cambridge, Kitchener, Waterloo, Guelph and Puslinch areas. Please click on a service below to learn more.

Housekeeping and Maintenance

Vacuum/sweep and wash floors; Dust; Clean kitchens; Wash dishes; Clean and sanitize bathrooms; Tidy rooms; Make beds; Change linens; Wash, dry and fold laundry; Ironing; Organize closets, cupboards and drawers; Water houseplants; Basic sewing and mending; Empty trash; Take recycling and trash to curb; Shovel and salt/sand walkways; Plant, water and weed gardens; Fill bird feeders.

Home Management

Sort mail; Schedule appointments; Help with computers & email; Assist with correspondence; Bill payment reminders; Arrange home deliveries; Oversee home maintenance; Plan outings and visits; Help with entertaining; Assist with pet care; Gift wrapping; Holiday decorating.

Handyman Services

Tree & hedge trimming, gutter cleaning, repair fencing & gates, screen repairs, install/replace smoke detectors, mirror & picture hanging, furniture assembly, painting, door handle & lock installations, replace lighting fixtures, caulk tubs & sinks, grout repair, etc.

Meal and Food Preparation

Monitor diet and eating; Plan meals and prepare grocery lists; Grocery shopping; Meal preparation and cleanup; Fruit and vegetable cleaning/cutting; Baking; Batch cooking for freezer; Monitor food expiration.

Shopping and Errands (For You or With You)

Grocery Stores; Clothing/Shoe Stores; Department Stores; Specialty Stores; Shopping Malls; Pharmacy; Post Office; Library; Dry Cleaners; Pet Supplies; Hardware Stores; Gift buying; Etc.

Local Transportation

Medical Appointments; Treatments; Physiotherapy; Social outings; Shopping; Beauty Salon/Barbershop; Religious Services; Plays and movies; Sporting events; Etc.

Personal Care

Dignity, preferences, respect and privacy are of the utmost importance when we assist clients with their personal care needs. We provide assistance with ambulation, feeding, personal hygiene such as bathing, grooming, dressing, and toileting. Range of motion exercise is incorporated in to most personal care activities.

Companionship

Friendly conversation; Discuss current and historical events; Reminiscing; Read books and newspapers; Write letters and correspondence; Cooking and eating meals together; Board games; Play cards; Puzzles; Crafts; Theatre/Movie outings; Dining out; Neighbourhood walks; Organize outings; Assist and encourage participation in social activities.

Respite Care

We strive to sustain family caregiver health and wellbeing by providing emotional support, and temporary care for loved ones who require assistance with activities of daily living, personal care and/or rehab and recovery.

Recovery Support

Assist nurses and other health care team members by following the established care plan; Measure and report vital signs; Clean equipment; Assist with passive or active-assistive range of motion exercises and other rehabilitative measures; Collect specimens for required medical tests; Monitor food and fluid intake; Observe and report progress, status and changes to the health care team and/or family members.

Daily Checks

Daily visits or phone calls to help ensure safety and promote good health and wellbeing.

And More!

If you would like assistance with anything that is not on our list of services, please contact us and we will do our best to accommodate your needs. If we are unable to help you, we will try to find you other resources.
