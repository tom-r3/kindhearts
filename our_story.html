Our Story

Kind Hearts: A Service for Seniors

Home About Us Our Story

Kind Hearts was created by Kim Matson-Robertson, a certified Personal Support Worker (PSW) who works with seniors on a regular basis because she genuinely cares and truly enjoys doing what she can to help the elders in her community. Kim has been a caregiver for over 15 years and is continually upgrading her skills in order to offer a top quality service. She has specialty training in Dementia/Alzheimerâ€™s care so that she can further help and support her clients.

Our goal is to enrich the lives of seniors by removing the burden of many tasks that become challenging and overwhelming as you age. Often, our assistance is the reason a loved one is able to remain living in their own home rather than move to a nursing home or assisted living facility. We help with the day to day needs that make all the difference, and give peace of mind to family members who may be concerned about the health and safety of those they love.

We also provide our service to assist in recovery after being in hospital, or when undergoing long term treatments. We provide respite care to help families find balance, and to relieve the stress caused by the fast pace and obligations of today's lifestyles.

"My initial introduction to senior care came at a young age, my grandmother was diagnosed with ALS when I was thirteen. It was a very sad experience but a valuable lesson, it helped me become the strong and nurturing person that I am today. I have the utmost respect for seniors and believe that a smile is the best way to measure happiness."

Kim - Kind Hearts: A Service for Seniors

While Kim personally assists most customers, she has a few helpers in order to meet the needs and accommodate the schedules of her valued clientele. All of our caregivers are fully qualified, trained and insured. We have a thorough interviewing and screening process which includes police records and driver abstract checks. Our company name was chosen to emphasize that caregiving is more than simply a job. Anyone can learn a skill and perform a task, but it takes a special type of person, with a kind heart, to genuinely enjoy helping others. Our valued staff are hand selected with this philosophy in mind. All of our helpers are kind hearts.

We are not a franchise operation and this means we are able to reduce certain costs, without affecting quality of service, and pass our savings on to our customers. We are a respected non-medical care provider and have earned our reputation by providing an honest and trustworthy service.

We are very proud of the high quality service we offer, and provide a money back guarantee that you will be completely satisfied. We consider it a privilege to help you.
