Kind Hearts: A Service for Seniors

Home Services Ideas

Our clients use our services in many different ways. These are some examples of the work we do and the ways we can be of assistance:

Mrs. Matson

Mrs. Matson uses our services 3 times per week for 2 hours each time. Karen visits her at home every Monday, Wednesday and Friday at 9am and starts the day by preparing a nutritious breakfast for her to enjoy while she watches her favorite TV show, Coronation Street. While Mrs. Matson is eating, Karen does a variety of household chores such as washing the bed linens, emptying the cat litter box, watering plants, etc. After breakfast Karen tidies the kitchen and washes the dishes. Depending on the day, and how Mrs. Matson is feeling, Karen often takes her shopping for groceries or pet supplies or sometimes they enjoy a cup of coffee together at Tim Hortons. Wednesdays are always reserved for vacuuming and taking out the trash. Every Friday Karen washes Mrs. Matsons hair and puts rollers in so that she will look nice for the family visitors that often come to see her on the weekend.

Mrs. Patel

Mrs. Patel has aging parents and they live with her. When she goes on family holidays, her parents prefer to stay home to give the family time to themselves. Mrs. Patel calls us whenever they go away, even for a weekend, so that we can telephone and drop by regularly to make sure her parents are alright and have everything they need. Now Mrs. Patel can enjoy her vacation without worrying about things at home.

Mr. & Mrs. Riley

Mr. and Mrs. Riley no longer have a car so they use our services any time they need transportation or have shopping to do. Debbie often does their grocery shopping and errands for them, especially in the winter months because they often find it too cold to venture out. They also call if they have doctor appointments or tests scheduled at one of the nearby hospitals. They usually phone us the day before they need our services, but at times they phone our office the day of, and we always do our best to accommodate them.

Mrs. Landrey

Mrs. Landrey, and her dog Sky, look forward to Kim's weekly visits. Kim spends a few hours each week helping them with many different chores that need to be done around the house (dusting, sweeping, cleaning the bathroom, laundry). Mrs. Landrey uses our service so that her daughters do not feel obligated to spend their free nights and weekends doing things for her. She always felt guilty asking for their help, knowing how busy they always are. Now they spend quality time together doing things they enjoy such as going to the movies or just talking while enjoying a meal together. Sky is always very excited when Kim arrives because he knows it means he'll be getting a dog cookie soon, and a good run at the park down the road!

Maisa

Maisa visits Mrs. Abdin every Thursday. She drops by at 10am to pickup the grocery list Mrs. Abdin has compiled throughout the week, as well as an envelope with shopping money. Maisa buys the groceries at the store(s) she was told to shop at. When she returns to Mrs. Abdin's home, she gives her the grocery bill(s) and any change that remains. Then, she puts away the groceries and checks the food expiration dates of items that are already in the fridge and cupboards - if anything is past the expiry date, she lets Mrs. Abdin know that she will be disposing of the items. She then washes and cuts the fresh vegetables Mrs. Abdin enjoys snacking on during the day. In winter months, Maisa also shovels and salts the outdoor stairs and walkway if needed. On average, Maisa helps Mrs. Abdin for two hours each week but she may, sometimes, be there for less time and sometimes more.

Mr. Wong

Mr. Wong lives alone and most of his meals consisted of frozen dinners or fast food from local delivery restaurants. Our helpers now visit Mr. Wong two mornings each week and spend that time planning meals, shopping for groceries and cooking and baking nutritious meals and snacks that can be frozen and easily reheated by Mr. Wong. He looks forward to our regular visits and often helps in the kitchen while we are there. Sometimes he gets a craving for an old favorite and we'll go out of our way to hunt down a recipe - his smiling face is our reward!

Mrs. Camilucci

Mrs. Camilucci depends on her Helper, Kim, to assist with her paperwork. Kim opens mail, records bill due dates on a calendar and books appointments (dentist, hair salon, etc). Kim visits her for an hour each month, and Mrs. Camilucci always enjoys playing a card game with Kim (they play Rummy 500 and keep a running score each time) before she leaves.
