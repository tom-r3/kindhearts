Frequently Asked Questions

Kind Hearts: A Service for Seniors

Home About Us FAQ
General

Why should we choose Kind Hearts to help us?

We are a respected provider of non-medical home care and our outstanding service and unbeatable rates set us apart from our competitors. We are fully licensed and insured and we provide an honest, reliable, top quality service. Our flexibility, professionalism and money back guarantee are just a few of the reasons our clients remain satisfied customers. Our assistance is offered and designed solely for seniors, and this allows us to focus all of our attention on offering the best service possible. We will always do our best to fulfill your needs, we are dedicated to helping and hope to make things a little easier for you.
Services

How do I pay for your services?

You do not need to worry about paying our Caregivers directly. We look after all of our employee wages, taxes and insurance. Our services are invoiced through our office and they are sent out twice per month. Invoices are due upon receipt and can be paid by personal cheque. If you prefer to pay cash for our services, please let us know so that we can make arrangements to avoid sending cash through the mail.

Do we have to sign a contract and commit to a specific length of service?

We do not use contracts. We have a "Home Services Agreement" that does not obligate you for any specific length of time. It simply outlines contact information, the services that have been arranged and our payment policy.

Do you provide your service to those living in retirement homes?

Yes, we work in all environments. Our services are tailored to the client’s needs and we offer many services that retirement homes often do not provide. Giving up one’s home and moving to an assisted living facility can be difficult, our caregivers are very compassionate and are able to assist with this transition.

Can I choose when I would like service?

Of course, we create our caregiver schedules to suit our clients' needs. You choose the time you would like us to arrive, as well as the length and frequency of our visits. We can provide our service on a one time basis, on an as-needed basis, or on a regularly scheduled basis. We are just a phone call away 519-807-4317.

How quickly can services be provided?

Arranging our service in advance is the best option but we are usually available to help as early as the next day, or even the same day if necessary. Customer satisfaction is a priority and we always do our very best to accommodate your needs.

How are services arranged?

Services can be arranged by telephone 519-807-4317 or email info@kindhearts.ca If you prefer, we can set up a no-charge, no-obligation consultation whereby we will assess your needs and offer suggestions that will best address your needs. Afterwards, you can take as much time as you like to make your decision without any pressure to accept our service.

Can I arrange services on behalf of my parent(s)?

Absolutely, services can be arranged by telephone 519-807-4317 or email info@kindhearts.ca or we can schedule a no-charge, no-obligation consultation so that your parents can take part in the arrangements. We will work together with you, and your family, to assess your needs and offer suggestions for care. Afterwards, you can take as much time as you like to make your decision without any pressure to accept our service.
Helpers

How do you select your Helpers?

We have very strict hiring guidelines to ensure the well-being of our clients. We have a two step interviewing process and thoroughly check all references and credentials. We require experience working with seniors and ensure that our staff have the people skills that are so very important in this field. Our screening includes a police information check for the vulnerable sector and clear driver abstract.

Do you train your Caregivers?

Our employees are very valuable to us. We have a thorough screening process and hire only the most competent helpers. Our employees come to us with a good deal of knowledge in the area of senior care, and have already had practical experience in the field. We further enhance these traits by providing effective hands-on training. On-site visits are done to ensure our Helpers are fully capable, and our clients are comfortable and satisfied.

Are your Helpers Insured?

Most definitely, all of our Caregivers are fully insured through our comprehensive General Liability insurance policy and OPCF 6A Auto Insurance coverage. We have a very thorough screening process which includes reference checks, vulnerable sector police information checks, and we require a clean driver abstract. All of our Caregivers are fully insured and bondable.

Will I always have the same Helper?

We try to always schedule the same Caregiver for each client, consistency is often very important. This allows our clients to become comfortable with their Helpers and, often, solid friendships develop. There may be times when your regular Helper is ill or on vacation, in which case a replacement would be sent temporarily.

What happens if my Caregiver is sick?

Any time our Helpers are ill, they are firmly instructed not to work. This is so that contagious germs are not spread, and our clients are not put at risk. If a Caregiver is ill, they call our office and we will in turn call you to see if you would like to reschedule, or if you would like us to send a different Caregiver temporarily. Continuity is very important and your care is our priority.
